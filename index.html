Wedding Details

We are so excited to have you join us for our wedding! The ceremony and reception will be March 22, 2025 at Lucky Arrow Retreat in Dripping Springs near Austin, TX. Expect a modern, laid-back, Austin vibe incorporating both traditional Bengali and American elements.

Recommended attire is dressy casual or Indian attire, so fancy up in whatever is comfortable to you. The events will primarily be in a covered outdoor space, so plan accordingly (see typical March weather in Austin)!

Travel Info

Please see below for all logistics surrounding the wedding.

Getting There

The venue is in Dripping Springs, about 45 minutes outside of Austin.

If you're driving from Austin, it's easiest to take either MoPac (Loop 1) or IH 35 south to US Rte 290. Go west on 290 until you get to Dripping Springs. Pass the main downtown area and go north on Bell Springs Rd, and Lucky Arrow will be on your left on the west side of the road.

If you're coming from out of town, it's easiest to fly into Austin-Bergstrom International Airport (AUS). To get to the venue from the airport, take 71 west when leaving the airport. This turns into 290 and takes you to Dripping Springs as above.

Accommodations

There will be limited accommodations on site. Details for booking on site lodging coming soon. If you prefer a less rustic stay, we recommend the Holiday Inn in Dripping Springs, which is about a 10-minute drive from the venue.

Parking

All on site accomodations come with 1-2 parking spots, and there should be enough parking on site for all other guests. Additional details coming soon.
